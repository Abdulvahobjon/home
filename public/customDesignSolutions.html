Kitchen & Bath Design Services

Thousand Oaks, CA

At ICal Construction, we believe that every remodeling project is unique. Our structured, client-focused approach ensures that each kitchen and bathroom renovation is seamless, efficient, and tailored to your vision. From concept to completion, we guide you through the process, delivering high-quality craftsmanship and stunning designs.

Our Streamlined Design Process

Step 1 – Initial Consultation

Let’s start with a conversation! Call us or visit our showroom to discuss your remodeling goals. We’ll outline our process, answer your questions, and determine the best approach for your project, including an estimated timeline and budget. Feel free to bring inspiration photos or existing plans!

Step 2 – In-Home Assessment

Seeing your space firsthand allows us to understand your needs better. During our in-home consultation, we’ll evaluate your kitchen or bathroom, listen to your ideas, and provide expert recommendations. Our $250 consultation fee is fully credited toward your design agreement.

Step 3 – Custom Design & Material Selection

Here’s where the transformation begins! Based on your vision, we’ll create a tailored design plan, finalize material selections, and provide a detailed project scope with budget estimates. Once you approve, we move forward with refining every detail.

Step 4 – Bringing Your Vision to Life

With the design finalized, we prepare a comprehensive construction contract, obtain necessary permits, and schedule your remodel. Our team ensures an on-time, on-budget, and hassle-free renovation experience—turning your dream kitchen or bathroom into reality!
